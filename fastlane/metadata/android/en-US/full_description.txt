Muzei Roma
A Muzei wallpaper source dedicated to the art of Rome.

Displays paintings, sculptures and views from Rome's greatest museums — Galleria Borghese, Capitoline Museums, Vatican Museums, Palazzo Barberini, Galleria Doria Pamphilj — with high-resolution images from Wikimedia Commons.

HOW IT WORKS

1. Install Muzei on your device
2. Install Muzei Roma
3. In Muzei → select Muzei Roma as source
4. Images are downloaded in the background and automatically rotated as wallpapers

FEATURES

* Library of over 40 Roman artworks
* Local image download (4K/1920px from Wikimedia Commons)
* Daily artwork or automatic rotation
* Full metadata: author, date, technique, location
* Direct link to the artwork's Wikipedia page
* Wallpaper sharing
* No account or API key required

REQUIREMENTS

* Android 8.0+ (API 26)
* Muzei app installed

All images are from Wikimedia Commons and used under their respective Creative Commons licences.
